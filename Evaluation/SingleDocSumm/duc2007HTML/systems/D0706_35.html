[0]YANGON, Myanmar (AP) _ Myanmar's military government has detained another 187 members of pro-democracy leader Aung San Suu Kyi's party, bringing the total to 702 arrested since a crackdown began in May, the party said Friday. [1]If the figure is accurate, it would be the largest number of arrests of opposition members since the nationwide democracy uprising in 1988. [2]More than half the arrests have taken place since Sunday, in an apparent government effort to stymie the National League for Democracy's announced plans to convene parliament. [3]The NLD said 194 members of parliament are among the arrested party members.
