[0]MONTGOMERY, Ohio (AP) -- Vice President Al Gore commiserated with those who lost their homes and businesses when a killer tornado tore through suburban Cincinnati earlier this month. [1]``I was truly inspired by the way everybody pulled together, neighbors helping neighbors,'' Gore said Monday. [2]``I was not fully prepared for how much damage there was''. [3]The vice president visited the areas worst hit when a pre-dawn twister with 200-mph winds carved a narrow path through affluent subdivisions in Montgomery, Blue Ash and other communities on April 9. [4]Four people died.
