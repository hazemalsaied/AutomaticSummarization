[0]Since they seized power in 1988, Myanmar's military rulers have used forced labor, torture and rape to maintain power. [1]General Tin Oo is a top general in the regime. [2]Officially, 15 anti-government groups surrendered as of January 1998. [3]The NLD party, led by Suu Kyi, won the 1990 elections, but the junta won't honor election results.
