[0]Newt Gingrich filed for divorce from his wife Marianne on July 29, 1999. [1]Marianne filed a counterclaim asking a judge to freeze the couple's assets, accusing Newt of transferring assets before filing for divorce. [2]Newt denied the accusation and a confidential financial agreement was reached on September 10. [3]When a judge granted Marianne's request for a videotaped deposition of 33-year-old Callista Bisek with whom Newt was alleged to have had an affair, Bisek asked another judge to overrule the decision. [4]Marianne asked permission to depose Newt's close advisors and associates complaining that he was refusing to answer pertinent questions.
