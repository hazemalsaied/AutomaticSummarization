[D0726_35] MONTGOMERY, Ohio (AP) -- Vice President Al Gore commiserated with those who lost their homes and businesses when a killer tornado tore through suburban Cincinnati earlier this month. [D0726_35] ``I was truly inspired by the way everybody pulled together, neighbors helping neighbors,'' Gore said Monday. [D0726_35] ``I was not fully prepared for how much damage there was''. [D0726_35] The vice president visited the areas worst hit when a pre-dawn twister with 200-mph winds carved a narrow path through affluent subdivisions in Montgomery, Blue Ash and other communities on April 9. [D0726_35] Four people died.
