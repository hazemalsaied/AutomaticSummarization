[D0716_35] CANBERRA, Australia (AP) _ Police arrested more than 100 protesters Tuesday at Energy Resources of Australia's Jabiluka mine development at Kakadu, a world heritage listed area in the Northern Territory. [D0716_35] The 112 were among 130 who entered a machinery compound at dawn, according to a statement issued by the protesters. [D0716_35] Of these 130, 20 ``locked on'' to machinery in the compound. [D0716_35] The protesters, who have been campaigning at the site since March, want development of the Jabiluka mine stopped. [D0716_35] The arrests Tuesday bring the total since the protest action began to about 350.
