[D0740_I-H] Expecting to travel for about two weeks, the two men aborted their attempt six hours after takeoff due to a hazardous fuel leak. [D0740_I-H] About a year later, the team (now also including an Englishman) resumed the quest but had to abandon their journey as China refused them entry into its airspace.
