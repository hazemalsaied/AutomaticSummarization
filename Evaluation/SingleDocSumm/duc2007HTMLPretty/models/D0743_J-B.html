[D0743_J-B] Turkey's own rescue efforts were ineffectual. [D0743_J-B] Official estimates were: 12,018 dead, 45,000 missing, 10,000 trapped, 33,515 under medical treatment beyond thousands treated earlier. [D0743_J-B] Worst affected were Izmit: 4,179 dead, 10,759 injured; Adapazari 3,046 dead, 6,884 injured; and Yalova 2,400 dead, 5,559 injured. [D0743_J-B] Economic loss was $20 billion, including the Izmit Oil Refinery fire, pollution, gas and oil shortages. [D0743_J-B] The Golcuk naval base was destroyed. [D0743_J-B] Power and communications were down from Ankara to northwest Turkey.
