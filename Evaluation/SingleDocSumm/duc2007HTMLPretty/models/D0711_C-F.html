[D0711_C-F] In 1995 the Justice Department blocked Microsoft's acquisition of Intuit, Inc. [D0711_C-F] In 1996 Justice investigated Microsoft's marketing and distribution of its browser software. [D0711_C-F] Microsoft allegedly offered financial inducements to computer makers for building Microsoft software exclusivity into their computers. [D0711_C-F] In 1998 Justice brought a major antitrust action against Microsoft for illegal activities aimed at protecting and extending its tight grip on the personal computer software market. [D0711_C-F] Sun Microsystems and Caldera Inc sued Microsoft for related practices.
