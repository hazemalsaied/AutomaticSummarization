[D0706_B-E] Myanmar has been ruled by the military in various guises since 1962. [D0706_B-E] After crushing a nationwide democracy movement, the State Law and Order Restoration Council took over Burma in 1998 and changed its name to Myanmar. [D0706_B-E] Nobel laureate Aung San Suu Kyi heads the popular opposition to the military government, which continues to harass and imprison members of her political party, anti-government ethnic armed groups, and student organizations. [D0706_B-E] The government has used forced labor and torture in carrying out its war against resistance by ethnic minorities. [D0706_B-E] Military bungling has turned resource-rich Myanmar to one of the world's poorest countries.
