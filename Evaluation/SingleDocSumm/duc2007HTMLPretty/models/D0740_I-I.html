[D0740_I-I] Bertrand Piccard and Wim Verstraeten attempted the first balloon circumnavigation of the earth in January 1997 aboard the Breitling Orbiter, leaving from Lake Geneva. [D0740_I-I] They abandoned the attempt after six hours when fuel leaked into the cabin. [D0740_I-I] Piccard, Verstraeten, and Andy Elson tried again in January 1998 aboard Breitling Orbiter II. [D0740_I-I] They were aloft a record 9 days, 17 hrs and 55 minutes when China refused them entry and they missed the needed jet-stream winds. [D0740_I-I] Piccard and Brian Jones made a third attempt aboard the Breitling Orbiter III. [D0740_I-I] They crossed the Sahara in March 1999 moving steadily behind a rival.
