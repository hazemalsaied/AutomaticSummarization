[D0740_I-J] Bertrand Piccard from Switzerland wanted to be the first to circumnavigate the world non-stop in a balloon. [D0740_I-J] So far he has made three attempts, taking off from a mountain in the Swiss Alps near Lake Geneva with different crewmembers. [D0740_I-J] On his first attempt the balloon landed in the Mediterranean some six hours after take-off because of a fuel leak. [D0740_I-J] On the second attempt he landed in Myanmar when China refused to allow entrance into Chinese air space. [D0740_I-J] March 1999 found him on his third attempt picking up altitude and speed over the Sahara after chipping off icicles.
